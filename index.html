Evade.

Evade it is a simple game about evading the enemies. It is a fast paced arcade game where low precision is key to getting a high score. You can get coins and buy skins with them, they come in all shapes and sizes and we have a big variety of them . The game challenges the players by spawning more and more enemies, turning a simple concept into one that is hard to master. Evade is coming to iOS and you can find it in Gamejolt for free.

About the Developer

I am a Highschool developer that makes his games on his free time, normally after school, I like making little fun games that I enjoy and would like to share. I started programming 2 years ago and have developed various games mostly in Unity C#. I make the art on all my solo projects.
